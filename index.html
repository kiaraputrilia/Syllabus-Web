HELLO! THANK YOU FOR READING MY SYLLABUS!

This syllabus serves as the backbone of my thesis, Perpetually, In Fermentation.The syllabus features six different essays — it is a concoction of references both within itself, and to the broader body of my work. Since these essays are formatted and printed in various sizes, this online reading experience aims to mimic that. By resizing the window, you can view each individual essay in its intended format. Once you’ve selected an essay to read, use the left and right arrow keys to navigate through the pages.
